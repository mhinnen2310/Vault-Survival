# Vault Survival 1.0.31

This release completes the exact-block district selection and staff building sprint, repairs the physical treasury/cash paths, and replaces several command-shaped player flows with typed native dialogs.

Highlights:

- merchant NPC inventory editor with per-slot price dialogs and NPC-only shop payout collection;
- physical-vault station application fees, merchant taxes, fines, repair wages, and job escrow;
- role-focused district menu, readable law toggles, typed station/project forms, and non-locked status displays;
- consent-based TPA, rank-limited player homes, and mayor-defined district homes;
- exact block claims, selection particles, replaceable district NPC markers, merchant market building, staff tools/breaker/build authorization, and vanilla NBT schematic loading;
- fully reordered and documented configuration with safe preservation/migration of existing values.
